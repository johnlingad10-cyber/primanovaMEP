About Us

Primanova is a Central Luzon–based MEP and systems integration company driven by excellence, precision, and innovation. With a dynamic team known for being hardworking, intelligent, patient, and highly proactive, we bring your engineering visions to life with dedication and professionalism.

Our Services

  • Mechanical Works – HVAC, Fire Protection
  • Plumbing & Drainage
  • CCTV and Security Systems
  • Fire Alarm & Life Safety Systems
  • Structured Cabling & IT Integration

Projects

Our portfolio includes residential, commercial, and industrial projects across Central Luzon. From villa electrical fit-outs to integrated safety systems for business complexes, our work speaks for our commitment to quality.

General Manager

John Arvie Lingad

John Arvie Lingad brings visionary leadership and deep technical acumen to his role as General Manager of Primanova. With a distinguished career in MEP systems and integration, he is recognized for his proactive approach, intelligence, persistence, and hands-on leadership style.

His extensive project experience spans both the Philippines and the Middle East, including complex infrastructure developments, high-end residential installations, and aviation support systems. This international exposure enables him to navigate diverse technical challenges with confidence and cultural fluency.

John holds a Master of Science in Project Management from the University of the West of Scotland, equipping him with advanced strategic and operational project skills. His academic background is complemented by international certifications in fire alarm systems, demonstrating his commitment to safety and compliance in all aspects of engineering practice.

He is a licensed electrical engineer in Qatar and a registered engineer with the Philippine Professional Regulation Commission (PRC). These credentials not only validate his professional standing but also reflect his commitment to delivering engineering excellence across borders.
